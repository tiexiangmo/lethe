 Turns regions of the mesh where the material_id=1 to a solid block by injecting it into the constraints This is achieved by imposing $\mathbf{u}=0$ within the cells which have a material_id=1. In addition, solid cells which are not connected to the fluid by any means also get a pressure dirichlet boundary condition which fixes the pressure to 0.
