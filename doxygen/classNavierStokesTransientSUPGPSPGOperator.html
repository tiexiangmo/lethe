Perform cell integral on a cell batch without gathering and scattering the values, and according to the Jacobian of the Navier-Stokes equations with SUPG/PSPG stabilization.

This function performs a cell integral, i.e., the Jacobian of the discretization of the transient Navier-Stokes equations with SUPG PSPG stabilization in a cell batch. The equations are given as follows: (q,u) + (v,t u) + (v,(u)u) + (v,(u)u) - (v,p) + (v,u) (Weak form Jacobian)

Parameters
integratorFEEvaluation object that allows to evaluate functions at quadrature points and perform cell integrations.
  • (t u +(u)u + (u)u + p - u)q (PSPG Jacobian)
  • (t u +(u)u + (u)u + p - u)uv (SUPG Jacobian Part 1)
  • (t u +(u)u + p - u - f )uv (SUPG Jacobian Part 2)

Perform cell integral on a cell batch with gathering and scattering the values, and according to the residual of the Navier-Stokes equations with SUPG/PSPG stabilization.

This function computes the residual of the weak form of the transient Navier-Stokes equations with SUPG PSPG discretization performing a cell integral in a cell batch. The equations are given as follows: (q, u) + (v,t u) + (v,(u)u) - (v,p) + (v,u) - (v,f) (Weak form)

Parameters

dstGlobal vector where the final result is added.
srcInput vector with all values in all cells.
rangeRange of the cell batch.
  • (t u +(u)u + p - u - f)q (PSPG term)
  • (t u +(u)u + p - u - f)uv (SUPG term)
