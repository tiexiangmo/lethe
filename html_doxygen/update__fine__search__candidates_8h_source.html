30 /**
31  * Manages removing repetitions and adding new contact pairs to the contact
32  * containers when particles are exchanged between processors. If the contact
33  * pair (in adjacent particles containers) does not exist in the output of the
34  * new (current step) broad search, it is removed from the contact pair
35  * (adjacent containers), since it means that the contact is being handled by
36  * another processor. If the pair exists in the output of the new broad search,
37  * it is removed from the output of the broad search, as the contact is already
38  * being processed. This process is performed for many types of particle-object
39  * pairs : local-local particle-particle pairs, local-ghost particle-particle
40  * pairs and particle-wall pairs, particle-floating wall pairs and
41  * particle-floating mesh contacts pairs. Note : contact_type is important for
42  * local or ghost particle-particle contact only
43  *
44  * @param pairs_in_contact adjacent particle-object pairs from fine search at last time step
45  * @param contact_candidates particle-object contact pairs from board search at current time step
46  * @param contact_type label of contact type to apply proper manipulation of contact removal in containers
47  */
48
