Detailed Description

template<int dim, typename VectorType, typename DofsType>
class AverageVelocities< dim, VectorType, DofsType >

Class that calculates the time-averaged velocities and pressure \((\langle u \rangle, \langle v \rangle, \langle w \rangle, \langle p \rangle)\) and the independent components of the Reynolds stresses tensor \((\langle u'u' \rangle, \langle v'v' \rangle, \langle w'w' \rangle, \langle u'v' \rangle, \langle v'w' \rangle, \langle w'u' \rangle)\). The generated vectors are displayable of visualization software.
